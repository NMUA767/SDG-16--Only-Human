SDG 16: Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions

Promoting peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, providing access to justice for all, and building effective, accountable institutions at all levels.

What is SDG 16?

SDG 16 seeks to promote peace, justice, and strong institutions. It calls for a reduction in violence, access to justice for all, and transparent, accountable institutions that contribute to development.
